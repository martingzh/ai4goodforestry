Scheduled Tribes and other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act

Year: 2006

Executing Ministry: IN- Law and Justice

Land Use: Forest land

Summary: An Act to recognise and vest the forest rights and occupation in forest land Scheduled Tribes and other traditional forest dwellers who have been residing in such forests for generations but whose rights could not be recorded; to provide for a framework for recording the forest rights so vested and the nature of evidence required for such recognition and vesting in respect offorest land.
