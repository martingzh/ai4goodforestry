Agreement through which reference costs are issued for reforestation or restoration and maintenance for environmental compensation for land use change in forest land and the methodology for its estimation.

Year: 2014

Executing Ministry: MX-Environment and Natural Resources

Land Use: Forest land

Summary: "The purpose of this Agreement is to establish reference costs for reforestation or restoration activities and their maintenance as environmental compensation for land use change in forest lands. The annex defines the methodology for calculating the aforementioned reference costs."
