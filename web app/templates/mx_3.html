Program for Forest Development

Year: 2003

Executing Ministry: MX-Environment and Natural Resources

Land Use: Forest land

Summary: "establishes the Rules of Operation for the granting of support from the Forest Development Program (PRODEFOR), which aims to encourage forest producers in the country, through the allocation of temporary support, destined to the production, the productivity of forest ecosystems, the development of the forest productive chain, the diversification of activities that improve the income of the owners and / or holders of forestry lands"
