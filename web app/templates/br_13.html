Decree of 21 September 1999 creating the National Forest of "Ritápolis" within the State of Minas Gerais

Year: 1999

Executing Ministry: BR-Non-Federal, State Ministry

Land Use: Forest land,Farmed land,Protective land and buffers

Summary: This Decree creates the National Forest of "Ritápolis" within the State of Minas Gerais. It consists of 5 articles establishing the aforesaid forest area the purposes of which are: a sustainable exploitation of its natural renewable resources, protection of water resources, etc. Control and management of this forestry area shall be under the competence of the Institution for Environment 'IBAMA'.
