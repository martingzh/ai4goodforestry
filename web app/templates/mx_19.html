Law on climate change action in the State of Quintana Roo

Year: 2012

Executing Ministry: Committee on the Environment and Climate Change (State Congress)

Land Use: Forest land

Summary: This Law establishes provisions to mitigate emissions and achieve a territory better adapted to the potential impacts of climate change, establishing the framework for the planning and execution of adaptation policies, in accordance with existing scientific knowledge. These provisions are applicable to all sectors, especially the energy, industrial, housing, tourism and service sectors, agriculture, forestry and fisheries, transport, development
