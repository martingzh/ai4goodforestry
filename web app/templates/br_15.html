Establishing the state policy on the technical assistance and rural extension for family farming and agrarian reform of the state of espirito santo (PEATER/ES) and the state programme of technical assistance and rural extension for family agriculture and agrarian reform of the state of espirto santo - PROATERES

Year: 2018

Executing Ministry: BR-Non-Federal, State Ministry

Land Use: Forest land,Farmed land,Protective land and buffers

Summary: This Law provides for Rural Tourism and the Policy for Promotion of Rural Tourism in the State of Espirito Santo with the purpose of promoting actions related to the planning, development and strengthening of rural tourism. In addition, it promotes the products and potentialities of the rural sector of the State. Rural Tourism is defined by Federal Law No. 11.771 of 17 September 2008.
