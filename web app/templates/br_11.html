Decree No. 43.710 regulating Law No. 14.309 on the State Policies on Forestry and Biodiversity

Year: 2004

Executing Ministry: BR-Non-Federal, State Ministry

Land Use: Forest land,Farmed land,Protective land and buffers

Summary: This Decree, consisting of 6 Chapters, regulates Law No. 14.309 on the State Policies on Forestry and Biodiversity. This Decree regulates any activity related to forestry activities and wildlife preservation. It establishes requirements to perform forestry activities in light of a sustainable use of natural resources within the State and measures to be applied for the environmental and ecological conservation of natural heritage of the State.
