Management of Public Forests, Brazilian Forest Service and National Fund for Forest Development

Year: 2006

Executing Ministry: BR-Environment

Land Use: Forest land,Farmed land,Protective land and buffers

Summary: This Law is composed of 86 articles structured in five Chapters. It regulates public forests management with the view of granting sustainable timber production. Special attention is devoted to local community management, forest concessions, environmental licensing and forestry prices. Moreover, it institutes, within the Ministry for Environment, the Brazilian Forestry Service (SBF) and the National Fund for Forestry Development (FNDF)
