Law for Adaptation and Mitigation of Climate Change in the State of Chiapas

Year: 2013

Executing Ministry: MX-Environment and Natural Resources

Land Use: Forest land

Summary: Enforces Kyoto and UNFCCC in Chiapas, the "formulation and implementation of public policies for Adaptation to Climate Change and the Mitigation of its adverse effects, to protect the population and contribute to the sustainable development."
